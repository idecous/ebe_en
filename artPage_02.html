ARTIST & WORK
ARTIST BIOWORK

Brittany Segal

Is an emerging artist whose work takes on the abstract and chaotic turns of her mind.??She creates with a variety of mediums, each one taking on a personal meaning to her.?Born in San Diego (where she currently works and lives), creating art has been a foundational portion of her life since she was a little girl. It is the outlet that she loves.?The outlet that has kept her mind sane from the real world.?With beautiful continuity and an abundance of detail, her abstract pen series reveal a lack of definite planning. Each canvas holds different shapes and lines, and while to the viewer they are unreadable, each piece carries the air of a locked up artist, refusing to leave the studio until a piece is cohesive, sometimes continuing for hours on end. Her main source of inspiration is her surroundings, and the people she finds herself interacting with. Her outlook on the world is complicated, and while this complication is seen in her work, there is something calming and simple about her collection. Despite the chaos, there is a serenity to Segal’s work. An art teacher of Segal’s once told her that she was stubborn beyond belief, never willing to change her vision or style unless it came from a voice within herself. He told her that is the one thing true artists have. And with that, she starts with a blank canvas or paper, sits to herself, humbly and silently, and simply creates.?
