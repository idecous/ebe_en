ABOUT EVE BY EVES

Eve by Eve’s (EBE) is a dynamic lifestyle brand which emerged from the concept of combining luxury and practicality into its wide range of products. From daily cashmere basics, to fine bedding, to Swiss-made botanical skincare, EBE aims to offer quality-driven, timeless and practical products that compliment a wide range of lifestyles worldwide. To fulfill customers’ dynamic and wide-ranging needs, EBE has collaborated with other brands to offer a full spectrum of products, including womenswear, menswear, kidswear, beachwear, loungewear, lingerie, shoes and accessories, jewelry, skincare, cosmetics and fine bedding and home accessories. EBE’s first store in Beverly Hills is the first high-end lifestyle boutique in the U.S. to incorporate the Online- to Offline (O2O) business model. The O2O business model seamlessly links various online and mobile platforms with the boutique to enhance the overall shopping experience. EBE integrates innovative and interactive shopping interface with various digital devices in the store to create an online/ offline hybrid shopping experience. In addition to the visual and interactive experience from the inshore O2O technologies, EBE is collaborating with emerging and internationally renowned contemporary artists to create an interactive, creative and artistic showroom. By bringing together timeless luxury products with the unique 020 in-store experience, which is enhanced by the display of contemporary art, EBE strives to revolutionize the way people traditionally shop, creating a unique shopping experience for all of EBE’s valuable customers.
